Controls:

  • W - move forward

  • S - move backward

  • E - turn right

  • Q - turn left

  • Space - brake

  • Shift - activate boost

Objectives:

  • Avoid enemies, they will bounce the ball away from you

  • Finish within time limit of 120 seconds

  • Collect boosts for bonus points and speed increase

  • Score a goal!
