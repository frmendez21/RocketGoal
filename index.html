Controls:

  • UP Arrow - forward

  • DOWN Arrow - backward

  • RIGHT Arrow - turn right

  • LEFT Arrow - turn left

  • Space - brake

  • LEFT Shift - activate boost

Objectives:

  • Avoid enemies and barriers, they will bounce the ball away from you

  • Finish within time limit of 120 seconds

  • Collect boosts for a speed increase

  • Score a goal!
